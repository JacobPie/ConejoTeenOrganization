Our Mission

Our goal is to identify organizations and individuals in need, and assist in the most effective and appropriate way. In addition, the specific objective and purpose of Conejo Teen Organization shall be to provide teens with an opportunity to learn about, perform and be engaged in community service activities. We want to provide a safe outlet and positive culture for teens to engage with other like-minded teens and mentors.

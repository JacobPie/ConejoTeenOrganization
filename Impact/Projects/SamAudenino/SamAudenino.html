Sam Audenino, Mountain Bike Enthusiast

Sam is a sophmore attending Newbury Park High School, his story featured in the local newspaper, the Acorn. He was involved in a mountain biking incident in July 2017, leaving him with and "incomplete spinal cord injury." The hope and objective of treatment and therapy is to help Sam walk again. CTO donated to Sam's GoFundMe campaign to help with out-of-pocket medical expenses and renovation costs to update his home to handicap acces.

If you want to learn more about Sam Audenino, you can visit his GoFundMe.

Casa Pacifica

"Headquartered on a rural 24-acre campus five miles from the Pacific Ocean, Casa Pacifica served abused and neglected children and adolescents, and those with severe emotional, social, behavioral, and mental health challenges. Our primary service area includes: Ventura County, Santa Barbara County, and the Southern California/Central Coast region.

Casa Pacifica provides hope and help for abused, neglected or at-risk children and their families."

CTO donated holiday gifts, ranging from shoes and clothing, toys, electronics and bedding essentials.
